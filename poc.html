A Piece of Cake(2023)-Webzine

'A Piece of Cake' is a quarterly webzine that aims to share some discourse on the things which are not a piece of cake, that imperfect humans have to deal with in life. Participated in the planning and brand identity design, content design, and web design for this webzine.
Planning: Eunseo Hyun
BI, Content design: Eunseo Hyun
Web design: Cyan Bae, Eunjin Ahn, Eunseo Hyun
Web development: Cyan Bae, Eunjin Ahn, Eunseo Hyun

A Piece of Cake은 불완전한 존재들이 삶을 살아가며 겪는 결코 쉽지 않은 일들에 대해 작은 담론을 나누고자 마련된 계간 웹진입니다. 해당 웹진의 기획 및 BI, 컨텐츠 디자인, 웹 디자인에 참여했습니다.
