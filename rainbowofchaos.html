We Live in a Rainbow of Chaos(2022)-Album design

Mailing art/Album design project which is contributed by KABK students in pandemic.
Inspired from the mail arts of "Correspondence School", and the quote of Paul Cezzane.
The album consists of a total of three songs, consisting of vocal and visual pieces involving KABK students.
In 2020, when the pandemic has just begun, and physical communication with others was blocked, I sent music with different lyrics to each friends so that they could continue their music one by one. Each song's lyrics described a trivial joy or absurdity of daily life, and they continued the lyrics in a way that reflected their personal interpretations.
As someone who thinks that music has the power to make our lives a little more lively and entertaining, even in the chaotic world that we are experiencing for the first time in our lives, I planned this work with the intention of not overlooking the tiny and insignificant pleasures in our daily lives.
The lyrics for each song were reconstructed into lyric collections (or posters) consisting of visual pieces in which the participants expressed the lyrics themselves.
"We live in a rainbow of chaos." - Paul Cezzane

Design: Eunseo Hyun
Contributors: Aiym Zhaishylyk, Binnie Kwon, Cami Chebs, Hyeonjeong Joo, Kirill Noskov
