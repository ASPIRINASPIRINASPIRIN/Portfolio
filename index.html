  • [Aspirin/Eunseo Hyun] is a multidisciplinary designer with a passion for creating unique visual narratives and interactive projects. She combines familiar graphics with unexpected digital components and incorporates humor into her designs to create a strong emotional connection with audiences. She is also skilled in visual identity design and consistently delivers high-quality designs that meet the specific needs of her clients. Her goal is always to deliver innovative, engaging designs with a purpose. She brings a unique perspective and a passion for design to each project she works on.
