A poster made with the meaning of "ç¶­å’Œé–€ (Yuhwamun)," which means harmonize, the signboard of the gate found in Gyeongbokgung Palace, which I visited during the photo shoot.
What do you think Seoul looks like? The image of Seoul that I saw after capturing it with a film camera was truly colorful and varied. The stories of the space read from the many doors (or entrances) in the developed images all have their own force. Six non-linear locations in Seoul were woven together to create a shutter door poster constructed of parallel rows while considering the identity of this city and wishing for a cityscape where variety coexists harmoniously.
This work was made during the internship at Triangle-Studio, with the directing by Kisung Jang.
